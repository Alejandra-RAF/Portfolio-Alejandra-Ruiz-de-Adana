ABOUT ME

I am a Data Science and Engineering student. I really enjoy technology, although this was not my first choice of study. I would like to specialize in the field of data analysis, but I am still not entirely clear on it. I grew up and studied in Fuerteventura, but I had to move to Gran Canaria in 2021 in order to continue studying at the university. During my stay in Gran Canaria I have made friends that today are essential in my life, which together with my family have helped me improve myself. On a personal level I have become a more independent, hard-working and more adult person in general.
